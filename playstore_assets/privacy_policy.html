Autexel - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Developer: Autexel

Summary: Autexel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device. No account is required. No internet connection is needed after installation.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Autexel ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed from the ground up to operate entirely on your device without sending any data to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever.

The App does not collect, store, process, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Camera images or scanned text
  • Usage data, analytics, or telemetry
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

3. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera for the sole purpose of capturing images of handwritten text for local Optical Character Recognition (OCR) processing. Images captured by the camera are:

  • Processed in real-time on your device only
  • Never saved to permanent storage by the App
  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Never transmitted to any third party
  • Immediately discarded after text extraction is complete

4. Storage Permission

The App requests storage permission solely to save files you explicitly create and export (Excel spreadsheets and PDF invoices) to your device's Downloads folder. The App only writes files when you actively tap the export button. No files are uploaded, shared, or transmitted without your direct action.

5. On-Device Processing

All text recognition is performed using Google ML Kit, which runs its machine learning model entirely on your device. The ML Kit text recognition model does not require an internet connection and does not send image data or recognized text to Google's servers.

All data parsing, file generation (Excel and PDF), and storage operations are performed locally using Apache POI and iTextPDF libraries, neither of which communicates with external servers.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party libraries, all of which operate entirely on-device:

  • Google ML Kit Text Recognition - on-device OCR, no data sent to Google
  • Apache POI - local Excel file generation
  • iTextPDF - local PDF file generation
  • AndroidX CameraX - camera preview and capture

None of these libraries transmit your data to external servers during normal App operation.

7. Internet Access

The App does not request the INTERNET permission and therefore cannot make any network requests. All functionality is available without an internet connection.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from any users, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by children as it contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.

9. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed through a data breach on our end. Your files remain on your device under your control at all times.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
