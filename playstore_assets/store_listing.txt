Autexel - Scan to Excel and Invoice

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars - 73 used):
Scan handwritten text and export to Excel or PDF invoice. Works fully offline.

---------------------------------------------------
FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars):

Turn handwritten notes, tables, and bills into digital files in seconds.
Autexel uses on-device text recognition to read your handwriting and convert
it into structured Excel spreadsheets or professional PDF invoices - no
internet connection required.

SCAN TO EXCEL
- Point your camera at any handwritten data - tables, lists, notes
- Intelligent parsing detects data structure automatically
- Edit the recognized text before it becomes a table
- Add or remove rows and columns directly in the app
- Export as .xlsx - compatible with Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets
- File saved to your Downloads folder instantly

SCAN TO INVOICE
- Scan handwritten bills, receipts, or order notes
- Automatically detects item names, quantities, and prices
- Fill in company name, customer name, invoice number, and date
- Edit every field before generating - nothing is locked
- Auto-calculates subtotal, tax, and grand total
- Generates a clean, professional PDF invoice
- Saved to Downloads with one tap to open

EDIT BEFORE YOU EXPORT
Every scan gives you complete control. Review and edit the extracted text,
modify table cells, adjust invoice line items, add rows, or correct any
recognition errors before anything is saved.

FULLY OFFLINE - NO ACCOUNT NEEDED
- Uses Google ML Kit for text recognition (runs entirely on device)
- No internet connection required after install
- No account, login, or sign-up
- No data is ever uploaded or shared
- Works in airplane mode

OPEN AND SHARE INSTANTLY
After exporting, tap "Open File" to view your spreadsheet or invoice
immediately. Files are saved to your Downloads folder and can be shared
with any app on your device.

WHO IS THIS FOR
- Small business owners who write orders or bills by hand
- Students who want to digitize handwritten class notes or data
- Accountants and bookkeepers digitizing paper records
- Field workers collecting data without connectivity
- Anyone who writes things down and wants them in digital format

SUPPORTED OUTPUT FORMATS
- Excel: .xlsx (opens in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, LibreOffice)
- Invoice: PDF (professional layout, printable, shareable)

PERMISSIONS USED
- Camera: required to capture images for text recognition
- Storage: required to save exported files to your Downloads folder

REQUIREMENTS
- Android 8.0 (API level 26) or higher
- Camera hardware
